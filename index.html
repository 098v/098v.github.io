About Omar

Aspiring Graphic Designer. 3D Modeller. Dude

Hey my name is Omar and I'm just your average everyday teenager with a love for design.

My interest in design had started during my freshman year of high school, but hadn't fully sparked until grade 11 when I had taken my first design course, Communication Technology, and with the help of my Communication Technology Teacher, Mr. Di Cesare, he helped me improve my skills. Now I am hosting most of my work on DeviantArt under my alias, 098v, and I have just finished my grade 12 Communication Technology class which has helped me improve my skills to reach new and greater heights. Services

Photorealism

Do you need something made that looks almost realistic, or how about some photo restorations done on old photos that have some wear and tear on them. Whatever it may be send me an email and we'll work something out.

Icon Design

Icons designed for whatever you need. Ranging from mobile icons, for your iDevive or Android Device, to simple yet beautifully made icons for your programs for users to enjoy. All made to match your specifications to the best of my abilities.

Logo Design

Starting a new company and need an amazing logo to match your company name and to catch the attention of people, or do you need a name and logo for your up and coming company? I will help come up with a company name and help make a logo to catch the attention of your intended audience.

3D Modelling

3D models made for whatever the occasion, whether you're an architect and need a render made to show to your clients or if you're an interior designer and need some images showing what the house may look like at the end I can do it for you.

Environmental Design

Need a render to match the concept art you've made for a specific layout of a park or other recreational service then I'm your man. I will render out your concept art in to something that will provide your clients a clear idea on what you will be providing them.

Web Design

Responsive and easy to navigate websites are easy to create for me. I can create your site to match your preferences and keep it light and with quick load times so that your viewers or potential clients don't have to wait ages for your site to load.

Photography

From hobby to professional work. I now do some photography for any occasion including, but not limited to, portraits, professional headshots, marketing and product photography. If requested I will also fix said photos to be more appealling and remove and blemishes and anything unwanted.

Print

Along with all of these other services I can also do some print work and design magazine layouts, book covers, advertising pages, and much more. All work done is created in A1 so that the quality of the work is not lost when being printed/scaled to fit certain page sizes.(Change last sentence to relate with Print)

Bedroom
Bedroom

Cinema 4D

Bedroom made through Cinema 4D with lighting edited with Photoshop

This bedroom that I have made through Cinema 4D with some editing done in Photoshop was made to test my skills with 3D work, to see whether I can handle a large workload without loosing inspiration and the drive to complete this project. This project was sort of an on and off project because of school work and studying for exams taking the place of the time I would use specifically for this project, so it took me about 3 months but once I had all my school work done and my exams done I had finished this quickly. The lighting and textures were created using the help of tutorials provided by GreyScaleGorilla everything else was made using future found off of Google/Ikea.

Throughout this project each object had it's own file, the sizes were'nt made to scale as it would interfere with the setting as each object's size was very different from one another, the dresser on the side for example would have been longer than the bed and cut out of the render almost half way through it so I had made is a bit more shorter and the plant was also shrunk as the original size was quite large, almost the size of an adult. Once I had everything set to the sizes that I wanted for each object I had put everything together and hit render.
